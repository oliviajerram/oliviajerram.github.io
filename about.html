Meet the team


Olivia Jerram, founder

I recently graduated from UC Berkeley with a degree in astrophysics and a passion for science communication. During my time at the university, I worked for four years at The Daily Californian, Berkeley's student-run newspaper of record, including a term as the publication's managing editor. Upon leaving the Daily Cal, I began writing for the Rothfels Lab on campus, communicating the team's research on plant phylogenetics to the public. I also work as a content creator at the Lawrence Hall of Science, writing and editing online educational videos for all ages. Now, I'm founding At the Roots — read on for more about the project.
